# Stage 1: Foundation, workspace, and baseline

**Goal:** Make the planning workspace and repo boundaries trustworthy before product work starts.

**Focus:** Validate what already exists, lock the worker contract, and keep plan/progress files as the source of truth.

## Features
- Feature 1: Repo baseline validation (`../features/01-repo-baseline-validation.md`)
- Feature 2: Worker contract and progress sync (`../features/02-worker-contract-and-progress.md`)

- Progress tracking can survive interruption and resume cleanly.
