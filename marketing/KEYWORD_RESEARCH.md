## Notes

- **Search volume matters less than intent** — a keyword with 50 searches/month from a desperate developer is worth more than 500 from casual browsers
- **Long-tail = easier to rank** — aim for keywords with 3+ words
- **Update this quarterly** — keyword trends shift, especially around launch
- **Google Search Console** will reveal actual queries once the site is live — use that to refine
